## Visual Design Overview

### Color Scheme
- **Primary Color**: Blue (#007AFF)
- **Secondary Colors**: 
  - Red (#FF6B6B) - Food & Dining
  - Teal (#4ECDC4) - Transportation
  - Blue (#45B7D1) - Shopping
  - Orange (#FFA07A) - Bills & Utilities
- **Background**: System Background (adapts to light/dark mode)
- **Cards**: White with subtle shadows

## Design Principles

### 1. **Modern & Clean**
- Minimal design with plenty of white space
- Rounded corners on cards and buttons
- Subtle shadows for depth

### 2. **Color-Coded Categories**
- Each expense category has a unique color
- Icons for visual recognition
- Consistent color scheme throughout

### 3. **Intuitive Navigation**
- Tab bar for main sections
- Clear back/cancel buttons
- Logical flow between screens

### 4. **Responsive Layout**
- Cards adapt to content
- Scrollable lists
- Proper spacing and padding

### 5. **Visual Hierarchy**
- Important information (amounts) in large, bold text
- Secondary info in smaller, lighter text
- Icons for quick recognition

---

## Component Styles

### Buttons
- **Primary**: Blue background, white text, rounded corners
- **Secondary**: White background, blue border, blue text
- **Destructive**: Red text for delete/logout actions

### Cards
- White background
- Rounded corners (12px)
- Subtle shadow
- Padding for content

### Input Fields
- Light gray background
- Rounded corners
- Clear labels above fields
- Error states in red

### Icons
- SF Symbols (system icons)
- Colored based on category
- Consistent sizing

---

## Dark Mode Support

The app automatically adapts to dark mode:
- Background becomes dark gray/black
- Text becomes light
- Cards maintain contrast
- Colors remain vibrant

---

## Animation & Interactions

- Smooth transitions between screens
- Pull-to-refresh on lists
- Swipe gestures for deletion
- Loading indicators during API calls
- Success/error animations

---

## Typography

- **Headings**: Bold, large (24-32pt)
- **Body**: Regular, medium (16-18pt)
- **Captions**: Light, small (12-14pt)
- **Amounts**: Semibold, prominent

---

This design follows iOS Human Interface Guidelines and provides a modern, user-friendly experience for expense tracking.
